1. What are Deepfakes? üé≠

Deepfakes are synthetic media in which a person in an existing image or video is replaced with someone else's likeness. With tools like Sora and Midjourney, creating fake videos is now child's play.

The Danger:

  • Fake news videos of politicians declaring war.
  • CEOs appearing on Zoom calls ordering money transfers.
  • Celebrities endorsing fake crypto scams.

2. Voice Cloning: The 3-Second Rule üéôÔ∏è

In the past, AI needed hours of audio to copy a voice. Today, AI models like VALL-E need just 3 seconds of audio to clone your voice perfectly.

Scammers take audio from your Instagram Stories, TikToks, or YouTube videos to create a clone. Then, they call your family asking for money.
